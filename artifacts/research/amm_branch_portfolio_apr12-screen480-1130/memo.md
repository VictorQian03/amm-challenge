3. Recent failures concentrate in three families:
   - calm relief that stays on too broadly
   - threshold or pulse defenses that remove too much resting protection

4. The next five branches should therefore cover:
   - one narrow refine of the current survivor
   - one explicit anti-arb branch
   - one weak-slice branch that meters rebates instead of leaving them on
